# Account Locked

## Overview

Your account may be temporarily locked for security reasons.

## Common Reasons

* Multiple failed login attempts
* Suspicious activity detected
* Violation of platform policies

## How to Unlock Your Account

### Wait for Automatic Unlock

Many accounts automatically unlock after a specified period.

### Reset Your Password

1. Click **Forgot Password**.
2. Follow the instructions sent to your email.
3. Log in using the new password.

### Contact Support

If your account remains locked:

* Provide your registered email address.
* Describe the issue.
* Include any relevant screenshots.

## Security Recommendations

* Use strong passwords.
* Enable two-factor authentication.
* Avoid sharing account credentials.
