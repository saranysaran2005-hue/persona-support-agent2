# Subscription Management

## Overview

This guide explains how to manage your subscription plan, billing cycle, and renewal settings.

## Viewing Your Subscription

1. Sign in to your account.
2. Open **Account Settings**.
3. Select **Subscription**.

## Upgrading Your Plan

* Choose a higher-tier plan.
* Review pricing details.
* Confirm payment.

## Downgrading Your Plan

* Select a lower-tier plan.
* Changes may take effect at the next billing cycle.

## Canceling a Subscription

1. Go to **Subscription Settings**.
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**.
3. Confirm your cancellation.

## Automatic Renewals

Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled before the renewal date.

## Questions?

Contact Customer Support for subscription-related assistance.
