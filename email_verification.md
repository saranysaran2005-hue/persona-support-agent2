# Email Verification

## Overview

Email verification helps secure your account and confirms ownership of your email address.

## How to Verify Your Email

1. Register or update your email address.
2. Check your inbox for a verification email.
3. Click the verification link.

## Didn't Receive the Email?

### Check Spam Folder

Verification emails may be filtered as spam.

### Confirm Email Address

Ensure you entered the correct email address during registration.

### Resend Verification Email

1. Log in to your account.
2. Navigate to Account Settings.
3. Select **Resend Verification Email**.

## Verification Link Expired

Request a new verification email and use the latest link provided.

## Need Help?

Contact support if you continue experiencing verification issues.
