# How to Reset Your Password

## Overview

If you've forgotten your password or are unable to sign in to your account, you can easily reset it by following the steps below.

## Reset Your Password

1. Go to the Sign In page.
2. Click **Forgot Password?**.
3. Enter the email address associated with your account.
4. Click **Send Reset Link**.
5. Check your email inbox for a password reset message.
6. Open the email and click the reset link.
7. Create a new password and confirm it.
8. Click **Save** or **Reset Password**.

Your password has now been updated, and you can sign in using your new credentials.

## Didn't Receive the Reset Email?

If you do not receive the password reset email within a few minutes:

* Check your Spam or Junk folder.
* Verify that you entered the correct email address.
* Wait a few minutes and try again.
* Add our email address to your safe sender list.

## Password Requirements

For security purposes, your new password should:

* Be at least 8 characters long.
* Include uppercase and lowercase letters.
* Include at least one number.
* Include at least one special character (such as !, @, #, or $).

## Reset Link Not Working?

Password reset links may expire after a certain period. If your link has expired:

1. Return to the Sign In page.
2. Select **Forgot Password?** again.
3. Request a new reset link.

## Need More Help?

If you're still unable to access your account, please contact our support team and provide the email address associated with your account. We will be happy to assist you.
