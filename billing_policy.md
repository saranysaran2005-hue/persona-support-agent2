# Billing Policy

## Overview

This Billing Policy explains how charges, payments, refunds, and subscription renewals are handled for our services. By using our services, you agree to the terms outlined below.

## Subscription Plans

We offer various subscription plans to meet different customer needs. Plan details, pricing, and included features are displayed on our pricing page before purchase.

### Key Points

* Subscription fees are charged in advance.
* Prices are displayed before checkout.
* Taxes may apply depending on your location.

## Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods:

* Credit Cards
* Debit Cards
* Online Payment Services
* Bank Transfers (where available)

All payments are processed securely through trusted payment providers.

## Automatic Renewals

Subscriptions renew automatically at the end of each billing cycle unless canceled before the renewal date.

### To Prevent Renewal

1. Sign in to your account.
2. Navigate to **Account Settings**.
3. Select **Billing & Subscription**.
4. Click **Cancel Subscription**.

Your subscription will remain active until the current billing period ends.

## Failed Payments

If a payment cannot be processed:

* We may attempt to charge the payment method again.
* You may receive email notifications regarding the failed payment.
* Account access may be limited until payment is successfully completed.

Please ensure your payment information is accurate and up to date.

## Refund Policy

Refund eligibility depends on the product or service purchased.

### Eligible Refund Scenarios

* Duplicate charges
* Billing errors
* Service issues covered by our refund guarantee

### Non-Refundable Situations

* Partial subscription periods already used
* Promotional or discounted purchases (unless required by law)
* Violations of our Terms of Service

To request a refund, contact our support team with your account and transaction details.

## Invoice Access

Customers can access invoices and payment history by:

1. Logging into their account.
2. Opening **Billing Settings**.
3. Selecting **Payment History**.

Invoices can be downloaded for personal or business records.

## Billing Disputes

If you believe you have been charged incorrectly:

1. Contact our support team immediately.
2. Provide the transaction date and payment details.
3. Allow our team time to investigate the issue.

We will review billing concerns and respond as quickly as possible.

## Policy Changes

We reserve the right to update this Billing Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page, and significant updates may be communicated through email or account notifications.

## Contact Support

If you have questions about billing, payments, or refunds, please contact our Customer Support team for assistance.
