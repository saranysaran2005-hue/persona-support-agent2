# Refund Policy

## Overview

We strive to provide quality services. If you're unsatisfied with a purchase, you may be eligible for a refund under the conditions below.

## Eligible Refund Requests

* Duplicate charges
* Incorrect billing
* Technical issues preventing service usage
* Unauthorized transactions

## Non-Refundable Items

* Used subscription periods
* Promotional purchases
* Services already fully delivered

## How to Request a Refund

1. Contact Customer Support.
2. Provide your order or transaction ID.
3. Explain the reason for your request.

## Refund Processing

Approved refunds are typically processed within 5–10 business days, depending on your payment provider.

## Need Assistance?

Reach out to our support team for further help regarding refunds.
